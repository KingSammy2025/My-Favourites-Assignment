Basketball

Basketball is a game played between two teams of five players each on a rectangular court, usually indoors. Each team tries to score by tossing the ball through the opponent's goal, an elevated horizontal hoop and net called a basket. Here are some other informations about basketball:

  • Basketball is played all around the world!
  • Basketball became an Olympic sport in 1936.
  • The NBA started in 1946.
